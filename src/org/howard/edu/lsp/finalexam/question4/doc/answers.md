# Question 4: AI Perspective Reflection

Over the course of my journey, my view on AI has changed significantly. I went from being someone who disliked using AI in coding, especially after a few "vibe coding" incidents this year, to really appreciating the workload it can offload.

I was recently able to deploy full personal Notion widget that allows me to play music through an aesthetic interface. By working with AI to generate the HTML layout while I handled the UI/UX design, I could focus on the vision while skipping the tedious parts of HTML and still keep the design exactly how I wanted it.

In the future, I plan to use AI primarily to check over my work and automate testing, as I saw how helpful it can be, like in Homework 6. I still want to maintain full creative control over my code, but I am definitely fine with letting AI handle the more repetitive tasks.
